Matthew Sliter is a Strategist based in Brooklyn currently working at Genius.

Genius' first marketing hire, Matt plays an integral role in the creative development and execution of Genius brand partnerships, contributing to over 1M in billings in 2017 alone. He’s worked on campaigns for Dropbox, Marvel's Black Panther, Nike, Showtime, Adidas, and many others.

Prior to joining Genius, Matt worked as a Strategist for Carrot Creative, leading strategy for a number of brands under the Unilever portfolio as well as new business ventures.

Loves: A Tribe Called Quest and The Chicago Bulls.
