Matthew Sliter is a Strategist based in Brooklyn currently working at Genius.

Genius’ first marketing hire, Matt plays an integral role in the creative development and execution of Genius brand partnerships, contributing to over $1M in billings in 2017 alone. He’s worked on projects and curated musical talent for Spotify, Nike, Dropbox, Adidas, Marvel’s Black Panther, Showtime, and many others.

Prior to joining Genius, Matt worked as a Strategist for Carrot Creative, Vice’s in-house digital agency, leading strategy for a number of brands under the Unilever portfolio as well as new business ventures.
